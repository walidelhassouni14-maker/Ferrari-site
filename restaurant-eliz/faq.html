Dietary & Allergens

Is all your food halal?
Yes, 100%. All meat and poultry served at Restaurant ELiZ is certified halal and sourced from approved suppliers. We are registered with the Berlin Halal Food Council and our certification is available on request. No pork or pork-derived products are used anywhere on our menu.
Do you have vegetarian options?
Absolutely. We have 12+ vegetarian dishes across our menu — from our beloved Hummus and Cacık starters to the Mercimek Köfte and various meze platters. All vegetarian dishes are clearly marked with a 🌱 symbol on the menu.
Do you cater for vegans?
Yes! We have 8 vegan dishes available including Stuffed Vine Leaves, Ezme, Mercimek Köfte, and Fresh Pomegranate Juice. Please speak to your server when ordering and they will advise on any hidden animal products.
How do I find out about allergens?
Our kitchen handles the 14 major allergens including gluten, nuts, dairy, eggs, and sesame. A full allergen chart is available on request from your server. Please inform us of any allergies before ordering — we take all dietary requirements very seriously. Cross-contamination cannot be 100% guaranteed, so we advise guests with severe allergies to speak with our head chef directly.
Are there gluten-free options?
Several of our dishes are naturally gluten-free, including many of our grilled dishes and most salads. We can also adapt certain dishes on request. However, as our kitchen is not fully gluten-free, we recommend speaking to your server if you have coeliac disease.
Do you have a children's menu?
Yes! We have a Kids' ELiZ Menu with smaller portions and milder flavours — including chicken shish, köfte, rice, and ice cream. High chairs are available and our team are happy to accommodate young guests with any special dietary needs.

Reservations & Booking

Do I need to make a reservation?
Walk-ins are always welcome and we accommodate them based on availability. However, we strongly recommend booking in advance — especially on Friday and Saturday evenings and for groups of 4+. You can reserve online here, by calling +49 30 1234 5678, or via WhatsApp.
How far in advance should I book?
For weekday lunches and dinners, 24–48 hours in advance is usually sufficient. For Friday and Saturday evenings, we recommend booking at least 3–4 days ahead. For private events and groups of 10+, please contact us at least 1–2 weeks in advance.
What is your cancellation policy?
We ask that cancellations are made at least 2 hours before your reservation time. For groups of 8+ or private dining bookings, we require 24 hours notice. Late cancellations for large groups may incur a small deposit charge. Simply call or WhatsApp us to cancel or modify your booking.
Can I book for a birthday or special occasion?
Absolutely! We love celebrating special moments. Let us know when booking and our team will prepare special decorations, organise a cake, and make your event truly memorable. Our private dining room is perfect for intimate celebrations of up to 20 guests.

Visiting Us

Is there parking available?
Yes! The Ottoman Street Car Park (P1) is directly adjacent to the restaurant. Parking is paid during daytime hours (Monday–Saturday 08:00–18:00) at €2/hour. After 18:00 on weekdays and all day Sunday, parking is completely free. We validate parking tickets for guests who dine with us — please ask your server.
Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?
Yes, Restaurant ELiZ is fully wheelchair accessible. We have step-free entrance, wide aisles, and an accessible toilet. Please let us know when booking so we can reserve an appropriate table for you.
Is there a dress code?
There is no formal dress code at Restaurant ELiZ. We welcome guests in smart casual attire. For our private dining events, smart casual is recommended. We simply ask that guests are respectfully dressed.
Do you accept card payments?
Yes! We accept all major cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Girocard. Apple Pay and Google Pay are also accepted. We also accept cash. Service charge is not automatically added to bills — tipping is entirely at your discretion.
Can I bring my own birthday cake?
Yes, you are welcome to bring an external cake. We charge a small cake-cutting fee of €2 per person which covers plates, forks, and service. Alternatively, we can arrange a complimentary house dessert for the birthday guest — just let us know when booking!
Do you allow dogs / pets?
Well-behaved dogs are welcome on our outdoor terrace (open April–October). Guide dogs and assistance animals are welcome throughout the restaurant at all times. Unfortunately, we cannot allow pets inside the main dining room due to health regulations.

Delivery & Takeaway

Do you offer takeaway?
Yes! You can collect takeaway orders from the restaurant. Simply order online and select "Collection" at checkout, or call us on +49 30 1234 5678. Collection orders are usually ready within 20–25 minutes.
What is your delivery area and cost?
We deliver within a 5km radius of the restaurant. Delivery is €2.99 for orders under €30, and completely free for orders over €30. Estimated delivery time is 30–45 minutes. We can also arrange larger catering deliveries — contact us for a quote.
Can I book a catering service?
Yes! Restaurant ELiZ offers full catering services for corporate events, weddings, birthdays, and more. We bring the food, the serving equipment, and if required, front-of-house staff. Contact us at events@restauranteliz.com for a custom catering quote.
Are there any delivery platforms you're on?
We are available on Lieferando and Wolt for delivery. However, ordering directly through our website or WhatsApp ensures you get the best prices as we don't pay third-party commission fees — meaning we can offer occasional exclusive discounts to direct orders.
