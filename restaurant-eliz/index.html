Our Story

A Journey from
Istanbul to Berlin

Founded in 2012 by chef Mehmet Yılmaz, Restaurant ELiZ brings the warmth and authenticity of Turkish hospitality to the heart of Berlin's culinary scene.

Every dish tells a story — from grandmother's meze recipes to fire-roasted kebabs grilled over natural charcoal, we honour tradition in every bite.

  • Family recipes dating back three generations
  • Fresh, locally sourced ingredients daily
  • Awarded Best Turkish Restaurant Berlin 2022 & 2023
